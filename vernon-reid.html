Biography

Vernon Reid was born in England on August 22, 1958. Lead guitarist of Living Colour and co-founder of the Black Rock Coalition who did a lot to shatter the color barrier in mainstream rock music. Mick Jagger was so impressed with In Living Color’s performance at CBGB that he invited the group to lay down a track for his album. He attended the prestigious Brooklyn Technical High School and then New York University.
