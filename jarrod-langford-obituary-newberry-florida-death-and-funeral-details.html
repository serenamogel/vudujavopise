Jarrod Langford Obituary, Death – Brantley Jared Langford, 20, of Clarksburg, West Virginia, died on Monday, January 7, 2019, at his home. On December 15, 1998, he was born in Clarksburg, the son of Matthew James Langford of Mount Clare and Amanda Wright of Bridgeport. Julie Langford Garvin, his adoring aunt whom he nicknamed Te, survives him.

For many years, Brantley lived with Te and considered his cousins, Jasa and Juliet Garvin, to be his sisters. His fiancée, Tracy Nicole Lawson, and his cherished daughter, Paisley Noelle Langford, both of Clarksburg, survive him. Cameron Langford of Mount Clare, Justin Whitehair of Grafton, and Joseph Tharpe of Summit Park survive, as do three brothers, Cameron Langford of Mount Clare, Justin Whitehair of Grafton, and Joseph Tharpe of Summit Park;

maternal grandmother, Cheryl Cockrell Erb of Bridgeport; paternal grandparents, James Langford of Mount Clare and Crystal Flynn Langford of Clarksburg; grandparents, John and Mary Wright of Clarksburg; aunt, Jennifer Tharpe Bodenstein of He also leaves behind many other second cousins and extended family members who were not mentioned but were dearly loved by him; close friends Tina Dye, John Garvin, Bob Loretta, and Sherice Holland; and people who had a special influence on his life, Bill Bennett, Basil Lucas, Billy Childers, Mark Jones, Scott Davis, and Paula Altman.
