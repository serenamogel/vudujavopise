Michelle Morgan (born July 16, 1981) is a Canadian actress and singer.

Early life

She is the fourth of six children.

On June 30, 2012 she married Derek Tisdelle. They have two children, Mara Carmen Tisdelle, and Noah Santiago Tisdelle.

Career

She is featured in the drama series Heartland as Amy Fleming’s older sister Lou and in the 2008 zombie horror film Diary of the Dead.

In January 2008 she appeared on Stargate Atlantis as FRAN (Friendly Replicator Android) in the episode “Be All My Sins Remember’d”. She returned to the show in the December 2008 episode “Ghost in the Machine” as the consciousness of Elizabeth Weir in a copy of FRAN’s body, making her the third actress to portray Weir. She is also the mother of Kate Kane in the new series Batwoman.
