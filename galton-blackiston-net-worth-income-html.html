Galton Blackiston is a Norfolk-born English chef. Morston Hall, his hotel’s restaurant, is Michelin starred and has three AA rosettes. It’s two miles from Blakeney on the north Norfolk coast. Sir Francis Galton, his progenitor, inspired his unique first name. Blackiston never had official training as a chef, instead learning on the job while working his way up to head chef at the Miller Howe country hotel in the Lake District.
