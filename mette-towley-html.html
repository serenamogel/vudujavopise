Mette Towley - Age, Family, Bio

Professional dancer/choreographer who rose to fame after being featured in N.E.R.D. and Rihanna's 2017 "Lemon" music video. She went on to appear in the drama Hustlers starring Cardi B in 2019. 

Before Fame

She studied dance at the University of Minnesota. 

Trivia

She became a member of the dancing group The Baes in 2014.  

Family Life

She is from Minnesota. 

Associated With

Songwriter/music producer Pharrell Williams hired her as a background dancer.
