Fedor Holz | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Fedor Holz is a well-known high-roller tournament participant from Germany who plays poker professionally. The Pocketfives website named him the best online MTT player in 2014 and 2015.

He won his first World Series of Poker bracelet in 2016 at the High Roller For One Drop event.

Holz’s net worth is expected to surpass $35 million as of July 2022. He amassed this substantial sum of money through a successful poker-playing career.

Where was Fedor Holz born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, and Education

Fedor Holz was born in Saarbrücken, Germany, on July 25, 1993. His desire to play poker professionally sprang from a lifetime fondness for the game. He finished his undergraduate degree at Saarland University.
