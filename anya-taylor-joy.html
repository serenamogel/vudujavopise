Anya Taylor-Joy Age, Biography

She is the youngest of three children born to a Scottish-Argentine father and an English-Spanish mother. Her father used to be an international banker, while her mother was a photographer and interior designer. She is the youngest of five siblings.

Her family relocated to Buenos Aires shortly after her birth, and when she was six years old, her family relocated to London, where she attended Hill House International Junior School. She later attended London’s Queen’s Gate School.
