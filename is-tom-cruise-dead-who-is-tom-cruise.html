Tom Cruise Career

Tom Cruise’s Hollywood career has been an incredible adventure. He began his acting career in the 1980s and, at the age of 18, relocated to New York City. He steadily made his name with parts in movies like “Top Gun” and “Risky Business,” becoming a superstar by 1986. In films like “The Color of Money” and “Rain Man” from the late 1980s, Cruise demonstrated his versatility in dramatic roles.

He won a Golden Globe in the 1990s for his commercial success in “A Few Good Men” and “Jerry Maguire,” as well as critical acclaim for “Born on the Fourth of July.” In the new millennium, Cruise demonstrated his action skills in “Mission: Impossible” and his acting abilities in films such as “Collateral” and “The Last Samurai.”

With the “Mission: Impossible” series in the 2010s, he made a comeback as an action star, and in 2022, “Top Gun: Maverick” became his highest-grossing movie.
