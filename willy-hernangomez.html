Biography

Willy Hernangomez was born in Madrid, Spain on May 27, 1994. 6’10” forward/center who signed with Real Madrid in 2011. He was selected 35th overall in the 2015 NBA Draft by the Philadelphia 76ers, then traded to the New York Knicks. He debuted with the Knicks during the 2016-17 NBA season. He played alongside fellow 2015 NBA draft pick Kristaps Porzingis on CB Sevilla. He won a gold medal with the Spanish team at the 2011 FIBA Europe U-18 Championship in Poland.
