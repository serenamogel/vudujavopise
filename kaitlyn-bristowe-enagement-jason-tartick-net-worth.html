Kaitlyn Bristowe is a Canadian television personality, podcast host, and former spin class instructor best known for her role as a contestant on the nineteenth season of ABC’s The Bachelor and as the lead on the eleventh season of The Bachelorette.

Who Is Kaitlyn Bristowe?

Kaitlyn Bristowe was born on 19 June 1985 in Leduc, Canada as Kaitlyn Dawn Bristowe to parents Leslie Hipkin and Mike Bristowe. She has a sister named Haley. She inherited her ballerina skills from her mother then got a dance scholarship and moved to Vancouver, British Columbia when she was older; And later became a spin-class instructor.

After Bristowe has made appearance in several The Bachelorette to Dancing With The Stars, she has shown off her sense of humor and quirky personality which stands out from rest of the contestants on the show that has made her loved by the audiences and the viewers.

The 35-year-old personality stands at the height of 5 ft 3 in or 1.60 m and weighs around 54 kg or 119 lbs. She has a slim body appearance with a humorous personality.

Kaitlyn Bristowe Is Engaged To Jason Tartick

Kaitlyn is engaged to former “Bachelorette” contestant Jason Tartick. Jason was the third runner-up during Becca Kufrin’s season of The Bachelorette in 2018.

Tartick posted the clip of the proposal in his TikTok page, captioning, “Just the reaction I was hoping for, engaged to the love of my life.”
