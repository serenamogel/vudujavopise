Who is Anna Shaffer?

Anna Shaffer is an English actress.

She is best known for her works in ‘Hollyoaks’, ‘Harry Potter’, ‘The Witcher, etc.

Anna Shaffer: Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Childhood, Ethnicity

Anna Shaffer was born on 15 March 1992, in London, England. As of 2023, her age is 31.

Her mother’s name is Maggie Barth but there is no information about the identity of her father.

He does feature regularly on her social media accounts. She also has a sibling, a younger brother named Josh Shaffer.

Anna Shaffer spent her childhood days in London, England. Her ethnicity is English.

Education: School/College, University

Talking about her educational background, Anna Shaffer attended secondary school at Highgate Wood School in Haringey, London, which specializes in Arts from Year 7 to Year 11.

Following that, she moved to Camden School For Girls to complete her education in the 6th form in both years, 12 and 13.

Anna Shaffer: Movies, TV Shows

Anna Shaffer first came to the spotlight for her portrayal of Romilda Vane in the Harry Potter series.

She was cast in the sixth part of the movie series, Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince on 19 December 2007.

Her character, Romilda was a fourth-year student who tried to use a love potion on the lead character, Harry Potter.

Anna Shaffer reprised the role in the two-part ending of the franchise, Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1, and in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.

In 2010, she joined the cast of the soap opera, ‘Hollyoaks’ as Ruby. Her character was the sister of Duncan Button.

Her on-screen debut as Ruby came in the episode broadcast on 3 January 2011.

In May 2011, there were rumors that she would be leaving the soap opera, which was denied.

She was featured in the series for a few more years. In October 2013, she revealed that she would be leaving Hollyoaks in the new year as a Digital Spy interview.

In 2014, Anna Shaffer left Hollyoaks but she did have a brief comeback in November 2017 and April 2018.

She was featured in the music video of ‘YDEK’ by ‘Warehouse Republic in 2012.

In 2014 Anna Shaffer was featured in an episode of ‘Glue’ as ‘Heather’. She also had roles in a couple of ‘Class’ episodes in 2016 as Rachel.

In December 2019, she appeared as Triss Merigold in the Netflix series, The Witcher starring Henry Cavill, Freya Allan, and Anya Chalotra.

The first season, consisting of eight episodes, was released on December 20, 2019.

Her character, Triss is a sorceress and one of the important characters of the Witcher saga. The show became one of the best shows ever by Netflix and was received well by critics.
