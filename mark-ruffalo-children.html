Mark Ruffalo career

Ruffalo started acting in the early 1990s and first received praise for his performances in the drama films You Can Count on Me (2000) and This Is Our Youth (1998) by Kenneth Lonergan.

He then went on to star in the thrillers In the Cut (2003), Zodiac (2007), and Shutter Island (2010) in addition to the romantic comedies 13 Going on 30 (2004) and Just Like Heaven (2005).

For his supporting part in the 2006 Broadway revival of Awake and Sing!, he was nominated for a Tony Award. Since 2012, Ruffalo has received recognition for his role as Bruce Banner/Hulk in the Marvel Cinematic Universe superhero series.
