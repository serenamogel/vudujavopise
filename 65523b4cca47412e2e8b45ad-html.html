Miss Universe Preliminary 2023: When are finalists announced and how to watch it?

The Miss Universe competition is nearing its grand finale, with over 90 delegates from around the world arriving in El Salvador for the 72nd edition.

Excitement has grown as the Miss Universe organization disclosed details about key events, including the preliminary competition on November 15, featuring the swimsuit and evening gown segments, and the national costume show on November 16.

This year's Miss Universe marks a historic milestone by welcoming mothers and married women as contestants for the first time, a shift towards inclusivity and diversity.

The age requirement remains 18-28, demonstrating the organization's commitment to breaking traditional norms. A total of 85 exceptional women will compete for the crown, representing their countries with pride.

Notably, Maria Camila Avella Montanez of Colombia and Michelle Cohn of Guatemala will make history as the first mothers and married women in the competition, redefining the image of a Miss Universe contestant.

Rikkie Valerie Kolle from the Netherlands and Marina Machete from Portugal will also contribute to the historic narrative as the second and third transwomen to compete in Miss Universe, following Angela Ponce in 2018. In a groundbreaking moment, Pakistan will make its debut in the competition, represented by Erica Robin, highlighting a significant step toward global inclusivity.

The 2023 Miss Universe is poised to set new standards of beauty, empowerment, and diversity. The coronation night on November 18, 2023, promises to be a historic occasion, symbolizing a transformative era in the Miss Universe pageant's legacy.

When are finalists announced and how to watch it?

The Miss Universe preliminary competition is scheduled for November 15 at 20:00 EST / 19:00 CST, followed by the national costume competition on November 16 at 21:00 EST / 20:00 CST.

These events will be easily accessible to a global audience through a partnership with Live Bash, offering a livestream of both competitions. Viewers worldwide can purchase a ticket for $5 to enjoy these spectacular shows from the comfort of their homes.
