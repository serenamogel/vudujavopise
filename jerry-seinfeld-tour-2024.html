Jerry Seinfeld Returns! How to Get Tickets for His 2024 Comedy Tour

Fans of comedy legend Jerry Seinfeld, get ready. The iconic comedian, famed for his hit sitcom Seinfeld, has just released dates for his 2024 tour. The comedic icon will return to the stage on May 10 with his first stop in Ontario, Canada. Seinfeld will make his way through North America, Australia, New Zealand, and Singapore. His final show for this tour is set to be in Atlantic City, NJ, on December 6.

Known for his sharp wit, observational humor of everyday occurrences, and timeless comedic style, Seinfeld's live performances are always highly anticipated. You haven’t seen him regularly on screen since his sitcom wrapped up in 1998, so catching him on tour is the best way to get your Seinfeld fix.

Seinfeld has continued to perform stand-up comedy to sold-out crowds around the world, solidifying his status as one of the greatest comedians of all time. And yes, during his shows, he will go on about nothing — what he's famous for doing so perfectly. It's his comedic genius and magic, after all. But since his tours remain so popular, you’ll need to be ahead of the game to snag your tickets.

How to Get Tickets to Jerry Seinfeld’s 2024 Comedy Tour

Tickets for Jerry Seinfeld's 2024 might sell out, so be sure to secure yours as soon as you know you can make it. Whether you're a die-hard fan or simply looking for a night of laughter and fun, Seinfeld's live show is guaranteed to deliver an unforgettable experience that will leave you smiling long after the final curtain call.

You can get tickets now at Ticketmaster both on presale, regular sale, and resale (depending on the date). However, if you’re picky about your section, you may need to hit up resale sites for the seats you want. Navigate around StubHub, Vivid Seats, and SeatGeek to find the perfect spot.

How Much You'll Spend on Jerry Seinfeld’s Tour Tickets

Depending on the city and date, Seinfeld tickets range in value. If you’re planning to go to his final show in Atlantic City, for example, a front-row seat will set you back $1635 on Ticketmaster, while a seat in the very back of the venue runs around $200.

If you’re planning to see Seinfeld in Vegas, seats in the best sections are going for around $400 on Ticketmaster. Sections all the way in the back are roughly $100.

Resale tickets on StubHub start at $72 for his show in Kentucky, $134 for his Canada dates, and $236 for his Delaware event.

What to Expect from Seinfeld's Live Show

Jerry Seinfeld's live performances are a masterclass in observational humor. He hilariously dissects the absurdities of everyday life with razor-sharp precision. From the mundane to the extraordinary, Seinfeld's keen observations and witty observations leave audiences laughing out loud and nodding in agreement — like when he points out how awkward or inappropriate people could be! (You can relate!)

He’ll probably riff on relationships, technology, or the quirks of modern society. Regardless of his content, you’ll find a relatable and endlessly entertaining show.

As far as opening acts go, we’re not entirely sure. Mark Schiff has been Seinfeld’s longtime opening act, so perhaps he’ll make an appearance before the legend hits the stage. We’ll know soon enough come the first show on this particular tour on May 10.
