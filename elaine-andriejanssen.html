Married To An Entrepreneur Eduardo Saverin

Elaine Andriejanssen fell in love with an entrepreneur from Brazil. The man who swept her off her feet is Eduardo Saverin. These two tied the knot on June 25, 2015. So, how did these two love stories start? 

The Indonesian-Chinese woman and Brazilian man's love story started in Massachusetts in their college days. The two dated for a while before realizing they were the one for each other. They took their relationship to the next level by officially engaging with each other on March 27, 2014. That's the same month Savannah Guthrie married Michael Feldman.

The romantic couple Elaine  & Eduardo has been living a successful married life since then. They have not made their marriage a subject of drama for the media. The two are living in Singapore.

Net Worth & Earnings of Elaine's Husband

Elaine Andriejanssen must have the best life. Not everyone has money to afford things they want, but her husband is a successful entrepreneur and one rich man. Elaine, herself hasn't disclosed her wealth.

However, her husband, Eduardo Saverin, has a massive net worth. He is a billionaire with a net worth of $13.4 billion. Bloomberg claims that Meta is where the majority of the wealth comes from. His net worth alone makes them one of the wealthiest celebrities couple.

The co-founder of Facebook Saverin spent $167 million for a home on two acres in Singapore in 2019. There is a pool, a tennis court, and a large yard. Saverin is also said to have a luxury penthouse suite in Singapore’s tallest residential building.

The man's success is not just limited to Facebook. Man has invested in many companies and has gained success. He also has a vast investment company in B Capital Group. His LinkedIn says that through this company, he has worked with many entrepreneurs and gained a lot of success.

Eduardo Loves Playing Chess

Eduardo Saverin is a billionaire, and it wouldn't take a lot to guess that he must be a busy man. But every human takes a break from work and tries to relax and enjoy doing something they like. Every people have their interests, and in the case of Eduardo, he loves chess.

The co-founder of Facebook, Eduardo, in the About section of his LinkedIn profile, has mentioned it. Besides chess, he is also into meteorology. In his profile, he has written:

And he doesn't just play chess. He gets competitive and is good at the popular board game. In fact, at the young age of 13, he won a massive match against a professional, and The International Chess Magazine even covered the incident.

Andrew Garfield Played Him On The Social Network

There were many things that the movie got right about Eduardo, but some fictional stories also showed up in the film. Popular YouTube channel Watchmojo has also covered it. They mentioned that movie scenes of Eduardo freezing the bank account were actual, whereas being Zuck's only friend and feeding meat to a chicken were made-up stories. 

Eduardo Is No Longer An American Citizen

Elaine's husband Eduardo made the news in 2012 when he gave up on his American citizenship. When the news broke out, the consensus among ordinary people was that he was just another capitalist trying to avoid taxes. 

But Elaine's husband has said that the reason he quit his US citizenship had nothing to do with that but rather because he loved Singapore and wanted to live there. He currently lives with his wife in Singapore and will likely grow his family there.
