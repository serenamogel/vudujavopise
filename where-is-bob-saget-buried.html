Where is Bob Saget buried? Burial site, pictures, Find A Grave

Bob Saget was American stand-up comedian, television personality, and actor who died on January 9, 2022.

He was born in the state of Pennsylvania and attended the Temple University. There he majored in acting.

Through Adam’s Eyes was his debut piece, and it helped him evolve into a comic genius.

He began by performing stand-up comedy at a variety of comedic clubs, where he met a number of well-known comedians.

Following that, he featured in a variety of television shows, the most of which were family or comedic in nature.

He was the creator of various works, including Farce of the Penguins and Bob Saget: That Ain’t Right.

Where is Bob Saget buried? Find A Grave

He was buried at the Mount Sinai Memorial Park Hollywood Hills, Los Angeles County, California.

His main role in the American sitcom Full House earned him the respect he deserved.
