Buy the Denim Jacket Travis Kelce Wore as a Nod To Taylor Swift

Dear Travis Kelce, thank you for finally initiating Taylor Swift's athlete era. The world is quite literally surrounding the musician’s love life as she appeared at the Kansas City Chiefs game on Sunday to watch her newly rumored beau. Yes, Travis Kelce turned heads as Swift and his mother made an adorable duo in the stadium suite — but he also brought a significant amount of attention to his trendy outfit before the big game.

KidSuper Bedroom Painting Denim Jacket

KidSuper Bedroom Painting Denim Jacket

With Swift’s 1989 (Taylor’s Version) being released in about a month — on October 27 — Swifties were quick to liken Kelce’s outfit to the upcoming album due to its blue and off-white coloring pattern. And that denim jacket needs to be in fans’ hands as quickly as sparks fly. Kelce sported the KidSuper Bedroom Painting Denim Jacket in cream, which sells for $295 and is currently in stock in all sizes except for medium (but you can get notified when it’s back on the market!). If you're afraid you might go out of style with just the jacket, then you can splurge for the matching KidSuper Bedroom Painting Pants (also in cream, for $286). The brand actually renamed the set after Kelce was seen wearing it to the Chiefs game that Taylor Swift attended — and that, my friends, is the hold that Taylor Swift has on the world.

During the game, Swift opted for a white Dóen tank top, black denim shorts, a red and white windbreaker (shoutout to the Chiefs colors), New Balance 550 sneakers, and tasteful gold jewelry. In typical Taylor fashion, most of those clothing pieces are already out of stock.

Kelce and Swift were seen leaving together after the game when a TikTok video went viral — and look at that, he just so happens to be wearing the ever-so-stylish denim set. Some might say the pair are enchanted by each other, or that they have no bad blood, or that Kelce is her new lover—we can go on with musical puns all day. But for now, we will stick to rocking our new denim outfit while patiently awaiting 1989 (Taylor’s Version).
