Career

His distinct style, captivating rhythms, and catchy rhymes have earned him a devoted fan following eager to learn more about the rapper. S.C.O.T., his third album, was released in 2013 and debuted at number one on the ARIA Urban charts. “Next Step,” Scott’s fifth studio album, was released in 2015 and includes the successful track “Next Step.” He is most known for his track “Next Step” from the album of the same name, which became a huge hit in Australia and across the globe.

He’s also made news for criticizing triple j for finally playing his music, which he saw as hypocritical given that they supposedly blocked his tracks when he initially appeared on the scene. Kerser rose to prominence as a combat rapper, going undefeated between 2010 and 2012. He immediately earned popularity for his distinct style and ability to deliver hard-hitting rhymes.

In 2011, Kerser released his debut mixtape, “Straight Out Tha Gutter.” The mixtape was an immediate hit, establishing Scott as a rising star in Australia’s hip-hop industry. He went on to record numerous additional mixtapes and albums, including “No Rest for the Sickest,” his second album, which peaked at No. 15 on the ARIA albums list.
