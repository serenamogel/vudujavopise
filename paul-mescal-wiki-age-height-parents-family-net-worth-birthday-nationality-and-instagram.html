Paul Mescal Biography – Paul Mescal Wiki

Paul Mescal is an Irish actor. He is best known for his role as Connell in the Hulu and BBC Three series Normal People, an adaptation of the novel by Sally Rooney.

His theatre work includes The Great Gatsby, The Red Shoes, The Plough and the Stars, Asking for It, A Midsummer Night’s Dream, A Portrait of the Artist as a Young Man, and The Lieutenant of Inishmore. He has also been involved in productions such as Much Ado About Nothing, Mojo, Children of the Sun, Three Winters, The Garden, Portia Coughlan, and Iphigenia at Aulis.

In 2017, he graduated from The Lir Academy at Trinity College Dublin with a Bachelor of Arts in Acting. He is also set to play Matt in comedy series Bump.

Paul Mescal Age

Mescal was born on 2 February 1996, in Maynooth, County Kildare, Ireland. He is 24 years old.
