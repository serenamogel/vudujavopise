David Denman Net Worth

David Denman, an American actor, has a net worth of $3 million dollars. He attended the American Conservatory Theater Summer Training Congress in San Francisco in 1991 and received his B.F.A. degree in Drama from the Juilliard School in New York City in 1997. His feature film debut was in the Warner Bros comedy The Replacements, and his breakthrough role came in the form of the character Roy in the television series The Office. He has played supporting roles in numerous blockbuster movies and recurring roles on many acclaimed television series. Denman is also an avid Shakespeare buff and has performed in dozens of Shakespearean productions. In 2017, he starred in the movies Power Rangers and Logan Lucky.
