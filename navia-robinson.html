Also, she was invited to attend and participate in their intensive 2-week boot camp which was held in Los Angeles in the year 2015.

Her television career launched with her amazing appearance as D’Asia, in the ongoing series named “Being Mary Jane”. In the series, she played the role of a young niece of Gabriella who played as Mary Jane.

In 2016, she worked in “Nicki” which was a production of Nicki Minaj. While Navia Robinson became widely popular after portraying the character in the Disney Channel family sitcom “Raven’s Home”.
