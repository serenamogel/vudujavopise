Rod Wave Bio (Wiki)

Who is Rod Wave?

Rod Wave is an American Rapper, Singer, and Songwriter.

His single ‘Heart on Ice’ went viral on Youtube and Tiktok.

Rod is better known for his introspective songwriting and combination of hip-hop and R&B.

Rod Wave: Age, Ethnicity, Parents, Siblings

Florida Rapper was born on August 27, 1998, in St. Petersburg, Florida as per sources.

As of 2023, he is 25 years old.

He holds an American nationality and mixed ethnicity of African and American.

His Zodiac sign is Virgo. His full name is Rodarius Marcell Green but professionally known as Rod Wave.

Rod’s parents separated while he was in elementary school. However, their names are unknown.

Education, School/College

Rod attended Lakewood High school. There is no information available about his further education.

Rod Wave: Professional Life, Career

Rod began his music career by releasing several mixtapes such as Hunger Games (2017), Rookie of The Year (2017), Hunger Games 2 (2018) Hunger Games 3 (2018).

On June 14, 2019, he released the mixtapes PTSD and Ghetto Gospel including the song Heart on Ice which went viral on Youtube and Tiktok also peaked at number 25 on Billboard Hit 100.

He released his singles: Popular Loner, Cuban Links, Close Enough to hurt, Dark Clouds, Misunderstood, etc.

On April 3, 2020, he released his second album which peaked at number 2 on the Billboard 200.

The song Rags2Riches from the same album peaked at number 12 on the Hot 100 and became his highest-charting song.

On August 11, 2020, Rod was featured in XXL’s 2020 Freshman Class.

His single, Cold December was released in January 2022.

Awards, Nominations

Rod Wave was nominated for the Best New Hip Hop Artist on the BET Hip Hop Award.

Rumors, Controversy/Scandals

There was a rumor on Twitter that Rod Wave was Shot or, dead but the rumors of the rapper’s death were false.
